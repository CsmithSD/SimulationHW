Community
The StarLogo Users Community is continually growing. If you have a course or project you would like included in this section, you can log on to our Design Discussion Area, where you will be able to post your StarLogo projects and get feedback from others. In this community you will also be able to see other people's projects and courses to see how StarLogo is being used.

Who uses StarLogo?

Researchers use StarLogo to model various natural phenomena.

Teachers use StarLogo to give students programming and modeling experience.

Find some information about StarLogo research.

Check out some other websites related to StarLogo and understanding complex systems.

Get involved in the StarLogo community!

You can join our mailing list.

Our mailing list is in an online database, and the mail archive lists all previous messages to the mailing list. This is a good resource for finding answers to previous questions asked by StarLogo users.

We also hold workshops every year for teachers. See the more information at the TEP Website.

Contacting the Developers

If you find a problem with StarLogo, please email bug-starlogo@media.mit.edu . We have several researchers working on StarLogo who can help you figure out what's wrong and how to fix it.

If you have other questions you can write to us at starlogo-request@media.mit.edu.
