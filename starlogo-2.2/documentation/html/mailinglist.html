Mailing List

About the List

This moderated mailing list serves a community of over 2000 people who share an interest in modeling and understanding complex systems with the help of StarLogo. Topics of discussion range widely, and include:

  • specific StarLogo projects that members are working on
  • general questions related to complexity and emergence
  • technical questions and tips about working with StarLogo
  • ideas for future improvements to StarLogo
  • concerns of educators using StarLogo in the classroom

Traffic on the list is quite variable: As a member, you may see anywhere from zero to 20 messages per week. If this sounds like too much, consider subscribing to the digest instead. This way you receive at most one message per day, comprising all contributions of the previous 24 hours.

To send mail to the members of this mailing list, address your mail to starlogo-users@media.mit.edu. If your message may not be of interest to others on the list, consider sending it to one of these other addresses:

  • To subscribe, or to end or change your subscription, see below
  • Problems or requests related to your mailing list subscription to starlogo-request@media.mit.edu
  • Bug reports, download problems and other technical glitches to bug-starlogo@media.mit.edu
  • Anything else: to starlogo-request@media.mit.edu

Managing Your Subscription

Please don't send subscription-related requests to the starlogo-users address. Your message will go to 2400 people who can't help you. Instead, please follow the instructions below.

Subscriptions are handled by an automated mailing list server based at MIT. You can join the mailing list and then manage your subscription at the Mailing List Web Site.

If you have problems with any of the above, or other questions about your subscription:

  • write to us at starlogo-request@media.mit.edu.
